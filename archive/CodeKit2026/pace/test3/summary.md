1. **Maximum Energy Segment**
   Find the maximum sum of a contiguous subarray.

2. **Most Frequent Secret Character**
   Find the character that appears most often. If tied, choose the alphabetically smaller one.

3. **Kingdom Travel System**
   Determine whether there is a path between two cities in an undirected graph.

4. **Shortest Route Finder**
   Find the shortest distance from city 1 to city n in a weighted graph.

5. **Stack Warehouse System**
   Simulate stack operations: PUSH, POP, TOP, and SIZE.

6. **Energy Pair Finder**
   Count the number of pairs whose sum equals k.

7. **Best Sales Period Analyzer**
   Find the maximum subarray sum where the subarray length is at least 2.

8. **Bracket Validation System**
   Check whether brackets (), [], {} are correctly matched and ordered.

9. **Robot Workload Balancer**
   Split tasks into two groups such that the difference of total energy is minimized.

10. **Largest Island Detector**
    Find the size of the largest connected component of 1s in a grid using 4-directional movement.
