# Numbers and Math Operators

## Goal

Practice using Python to add, subtract, multiply, divide, and solve small math problems.

## What students should know first

* Know variables.
* Know integer and float values.
* Know how to print results.

## Questions

### Question 1: Candy Addition

**Problem description:** Add two candy counts.

**Input format:** Two integers.

**Output format:** Their sum.

**Example input:**

```text
12
5
```

**Example output:**

```text
17
```

**Hint:** Use +.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 2: Cookie Subtraction

**Problem description:** Find how many cookies are left after eating some.

**Input format:** Two integers: start and eaten.

**Output format:** Cookies left.

**Example input:**

```text
20
6
```

**Example output:**

```text
14
```

**Hint:** Use -.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 3: Sticker Packs

**Problem description:** Each pack has 8 stickers. Read packs and print total stickers.

**Input format:** One integer.

**Output format:** Total stickers.

**Example input:**

```text
4
```

**Example output:**

```text
32
```

**Hint:** Use multiplication.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 4: Pizza Sharing

**Problem description:** Read slices and friends. Print slices per friend using integer division.

**Input format:** Two integers.

**Output format:** Slices each.

**Example input:**

```text
10
3
```

**Example output:**

```text
3
```

**Hint:** Use //.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 5: Leftover Gems

**Problem description:** Read gems and boxes. Print how many gems are left after filling boxes evenly.

**Input format:** Two integers.

**Output format:** Remainder.

**Example input:**

```text
17
5
```

**Example output:**

```text
2
```

**Hint:** Use %.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 6: Square Garden

**Problem description:** Read a side length and print the area of a square.

**Input format:** One integer.

**Output format:** Area.

**Example input:**

```text
6
```

**Example output:**

```text
36
```

**Hint:** side * side.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 7: Robot Steps

**Problem description:** A robot moves forward a steps and backward b steps. Print its final position.

**Input format:** Two integers.

**Output format:** Final position.

**Example input:**

```text
9
4
```

**Example output:**

```text
5
```

**Hint:** Forward is plus, backward is minus.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 8: Magic Formula

**Problem description:** Read x and calculate x squared plus 3.

**Input format:** One integer.

**Output format:** Formula result.

**Example input:**

```text
5
```

**Example output:**

```text
28
```

**Hint:** x * x + 3.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 9: Shop Total With Tax

**Problem description:** Read price and tax. Print price plus tax.

**Input format:** Two decimal numbers.

**Output format:** Total as decimal.

**Example input:**

```text
12.5
1.5
```

**Example output:**

```text
14.0
```

**Hint:** Use float inputs.

**Difficulty:** Hard

### Question 10: Race Average Speed

**Problem description:** Read distance and time. Print distance divided by time.

**Input format:** Two decimal numbers.

**Output format:** Average speed.

**Example input:**

```text
100
4
```

**Example output:**

```text
25.0
```

**Hint:** Use / for decimal division.

**Difficulty:** Hard
