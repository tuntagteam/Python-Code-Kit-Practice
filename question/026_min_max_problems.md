# Min Max Problems

## Goal

Practice finding the smallest and biggest values.

## What students should know first

* Know loops.
* Know comparisons.
* Know lists.

## Questions

### Question 1: Tallest Tower

**Problem description:** Read n tower heights and print the tallest height.

**Input format:** First line n, second line n integers.

**Output format:** Maximum height.

**Example input:**

```text
5
3 9 2 8 4
```

**Example output:**

```text
9
```

**Hint:** Compare each height to the best so far.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 2: Smallest Snack Price

**Problem description:** Read prices and print the lowest price.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Lowest price.

**Example input:**

```text
4
8 3 6 5
```

**Example output:**

```text
3
```

**Hint:** Use min() or a loop.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 3: Best and Worst Score

**Problem description:** Read scores and print highest then lowest.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Two lines.

**Example input:**

```text
5
7 10 4 9 6
```

**Example output:**

```text
10
4
```

**Hint:** Track both values.

**Difficulty:** Easy

### Question 4: Longest Word

**Problem description:** Read words and print the longest word.

**Input format:** One line words.

**Output format:** Longest word.

**Example input:**

```text
cat elephant dog
```

**Example output:**

```text
elephant
```

**Hint:** Compare lengths.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 5: Shortest Name

**Problem description:** Read n names and print the shortest name.

**Input format:** n then n names.

**Output format:** Shortest name.

**Example input:**

```text
3
Lia
Alexander
Bo
```

**Example output:**

```text
Bo
```

**Hint:** Keep the first shortest if tied.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 6: Temperature Range

**Problem description:** Read temperatures and print max minus min.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Range.

**Example input:**

```text
5
20 25 18 22 30
```

**Example output:**

```text
12
```

**Hint:** Find max and min.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 7: Nearest to Zero

**Problem description:** Read numbers and print the number closest to zero.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Closest number.

**Example input:**

```text
5
-8 3 -2 6 9
```

**Example output:**

```text
-2
```

**Hint:** Compare absolute values.

**Difficulty:** Medium

### Question 8: Second Biggest

**Problem description:** Read unique numbers and print the second biggest.

**Input format:** n then n unique integers.

**Output format:** Second biggest.

**Example input:**

```text
5
4 9 1 7 3
```

**Example output:**

```text
7
```

**Hint:** Sort or track top two.

**Difficulty:** Hard

### Question 9: Best Profit

**Problem description:** Read daily prices and print biggest difference later price minus earlier price.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Best profit.

**Example input:**

```text
6
5 3 6 1 8 4
```

**Example output:**

```text
7
```

**Hint:** Keep smallest price so far.

**Difficulty:** Hard

### Question 10: Min Max Positions

**Problem description:** Read numbers. Print the indexes of the smallest and biggest values.

**Input format:** n then n integers.

**Output format:** Two indexes.

**Example input:**

```text
5
4 1 9 2 9
```

**Example output:**

```text
1 2
```

**Hint:** Use first position if tied.

**Difficulty:** Hard
